Bio

General Background:

I have been privileged to practice law in Clermont County, Ohio and neighboring counties for more than 40 years.  From 1968 until 1993, I was employed by and later a partner in the law firm of Walker, Bradford & Hill.  In the early years, I was associated with my father James W. Walker (1908 – 1980), Raymond Bradford (1904 – 1993), and Ralph A. Hill.  My brother William Walker joined the firm a little later.  After leaving the firm, Bill enjoyed a long and distinguished career as Judge of the Common Pleas Court of Clermont County.  Other attorneys with whom I served over the years included Douglas J. Bradford, Allen L. Burreson (1936 – 2002), D. Vincent Faris, and John Korfhagen.  In the years since leaving the partnership, I have engaged in the solo practice of law.

Education, Experience and Professional Associations:

University of Cincinnati  Bachelor of Arts Degree, Economics and Political Science, 1964

Salmon P. Chase College of Law Juris Doctor, 1968

Clermont County Bar Association 1968 to present, Past President and Chairman of Certified Grievance Committee

Ohio State Bar Association  1968 to present

Admitted to Practice All Ohio Courts of Record and United State District Court, Southern District of Ohio

Past and Present Associations:

Montgomery Community Church, since 1999; Faith Evangelical Free Church, Milford, Ohio, 1992 – 1999; Batavia First United Methodist Church, until 1992

Clermont Philharmonic Orchestra, Board Member, 1997 – 2005

Clermont County Chamber of Commerce, Board Member and Officer, 1975 -1991

Batavia LSD Board of Education, Board Member and President 1970-1983
